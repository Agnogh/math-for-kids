Rules of the game



  1. Select your game mode (normal, easy, easiest)


  2. Pick your lucky number (1-9)


  3. Click on the Spin (I feel Lucky)


  4. Check your luck in outcome pane


  5. Keep your lucky number selected or select new one for your next spin


  6. After 10 spins see final outcome


  7. Click on the link to leaderboard to see your "Luck rank"


  8. Click on the "Start the Game" button below to get started
