Rules of the game

  • Select your game mode (normal, easy, easiest)
  • Pick your lucky number (1-9)
  • Click on the Spin (I feel Lucky)
  • Check your luck in outcome pane
  • Keep your lucky number selected or select new one for your next spin
  • After 10 spins see final outcome
  • Click on the link to leaderboard to see your "Luck rank"
